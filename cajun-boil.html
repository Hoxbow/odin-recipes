Cajun Seafood Boil

Recipe

A declicious Cajun favorite. This recipe feeds 6 to 8 people in 30 minutes of prep/cook time.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Shrimp (unpeeled)
  • 5 lbs Crawfish (alive)
  • 2 lbs smoked sausage (cooked)
  • 2 lbs small red potatoes or fingerling potatoes
  • 4 pieces of corn
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 6 oz Zatarain's Crawfish, Shrimp & Crab Boil bag
  • 4 fl oz Zatarain's Concentrated Shrimp & Crab Boil (liquid)
  • Slap Ya Mama's Cajun Seasoning (to taste)
  • 1 stick of butter

Prep

  • Rinse the potatoes, do not peel
  • Cut sausages into 3/4" pieces
  • Slice the lemon into 1/4" slices
  • Slice the onion into 1/4" slices
  • Peel and cut the corn in 3 equal pieces

The key to a successful cajun boil is to know how long each item takes to cook. When you know how long each takes to cook, you can add that ingredients at the right time so everything is finished cooking at the same time.

Cook Times

  • Potatoes = 20 minutes (add at beginning of boil)
  • Onions = 20 minutes (add at beginning of boil)
  • Corn = 5 minutes (add 15 minutes into boil)
  • Crawfish = 5 minutes (add 15 minutes into boil)
  • Smoked Sausage = 5 minutes (add 15 minutes into boil)
  • Shrimp = 2 minutes (add 18 minutes into boil)

Steps

  1. Fill a 20 quart pot a little over half way with water
  2. Bring water to a rapid boil
  3. Add the Zatarain's Crawfish, Shrimp & Crab Boil as well as the Concentrated Shrimp & Crab Boil liquid
  4. Add potatoes and onions to the pot, stirring occasionally
  5. Boil for 15 minutes
  6. Test potatoes for tenderness by sticking a fork in them
  7. If tender, add the corn, crawfish and sausage to the boil
  8. Boil for 3 minutes
  9. Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink
  10. Remove from heat and drain the liquid from the pot
  11. Melt butter and add to pot. Toss the ingredients to evenly coat with the butter
  12. Lay several layers of newspaper on your table and spread the ingredients on the newspaper
  13. Sprinkly the Slap Ya Mama seasoning all over the seafood boil to flavor
  14. Enjoy while hot!
