Red Lentil Soup

Recipe

A delicious soup that can be made in bulk and frozen for later

Ingredients

  • 2 cups red lentils
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red or green pepper (seeded and diced)
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 2 stalks celery (diced)
  • 1 yellow onion (diced)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
