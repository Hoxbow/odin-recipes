Split Pea Soup

Recipe

A family favorite that is perfect for cold nights

Ingredients

  • 1 lb split peas
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 white onion (diced)
  • 2 stalks of celery (cut in 1/2" pieces)
  • 2 peeled russet potatoes (diced)
  • 2 carrots (cubed)
  • 1 ham hock
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 5 sprigs of fresh thyme

Steps

  1. Saute onions, carrots and celery in oil for 5 minutes
  2. Add peas and potatoes
  3. Cover with water or broth
  4. Add ham hock
  5. Boil until meat is falling off the bone, remove, dice and return to pot
  6. Add water as necessary
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
